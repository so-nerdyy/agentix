# Phase 19 - Self-Healing and Self-Evolution

## Purpose

Turn repeated failures into reusable procedures instead of repeated incidents.

## Scope

- Failure fingerprinting.
- Procedure promotion.
- Rollback and rejection.
- Memory-assisted recovery.

## Implementation

- Capture recurring failure patterns.
- Propose and verify recovery actions.
- Promote approved fixes into durable procedures.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Old or deprecated procedures do not get reused.
- Successful recovery paths are stored and reused safely.
